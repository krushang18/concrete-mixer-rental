About M/S O.C.Shah

Your trusted partner for reliable concrete mixer rental solutions

Our Story

Leading Provider of Construction Equipment

Established Excellence in Equipment Rental

M/S O.C.Shah has been a cornerstone in the construction equipment rental industry, specializing in self-loading concrete mixers. With years of dedicated service, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and customer satisfaction across Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Maharashtra.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide construction companies with access to high-quality, well-maintained equipment that helps them complete their projects efficiently and on time. Today, we are proud to be a trusted partner for numerous large-scale and specialized projects, including solar power plants, RCC road works, canal lining projects, and a wide range of other infrastructure developments.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our business - from maintaining our equipment fleet to providing exceptional customer service. We understand the challenges faced by construction professionals and strive to make equipment rental as seamless and reliable as possible.
